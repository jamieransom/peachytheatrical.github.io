Peachy, Who?

Peachy Theatrical was created in 2020 to change the way shows are produced for the better.

Our Story

Since our creation in 2020, Peachy has one simple mission - to change how theatre is produced for the better. We take a people focused approach to producing shows - because a happy company means a happy audience. Our team members have years of industry experience and work alongside like-minded professionals to bring our ideas to life on-stage.


We have big plans for our first productions, so watch out for future news. Our audience's experience is at the core of how we produce our shows and we never take them for granted unlike many other major producers. Further, we believe in treating our staff and performers fairly to get a fantastic performance from them consistently!

A new kind of theatrical producer.
